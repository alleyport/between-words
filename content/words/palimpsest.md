---
title: "palimpsest"
definition: "Something reused but still bearing visible traces of earlier forms."
tag: "memory"
etymology: "From Greek palimpsestos, scraped again; through Latin and French into English."
---
History: Originally a manuscript page scraped clean for reuse.

Personal reflection: Cities are palimpsests; so are personal habits.

Example usage: Her notebook looked like a palimpsest of revisions and second thoughts.
