---
title: "saudade"
definition: "A deep, melancholic longing for someone or something absent."
tag: "emotion"
etymology: "From Portuguese, with roots connected to solitude and longing traditions."
---
History: Central to Portuguese and Brazilian poetics, especially in fado and lyric traditions.

Personal reflection: Saudade feels less like loss than like a room where memory still speaks.

Example usage: The melody carried saudade long after the last note.
